About Jewelry Planners

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Jewelry Planners began as a small workshop with a grand vision: to create jewelry that tells a story. For nearly four decades, we've been transforming raw materials into pieces that capture life's most precious moments.

We personally source and purchase raw gold and precious stones from trusted suppliers worldwide, ensuring every material meets our exacting standards. Our master craftsmen combine traditional techniques with contemporary design, ensuring each piece is both timeless and modern. From sourcing the finest materials to the final polish, every step reflects our dedication to perfection.

Our Services

Custom Jewelry Design

Bring your vision to life with our bespoke jewelry design service. Our expert designers work closely with you to create one-of-a-kind pieces that reflect your personal style.

  • Personal consultation
  • 3D design rendering
  • Material selection guidance
  • Prototype creation

Gold Crafting

We purchase raw gold bullion and transform it into stunning jewelry pieces. Using traditional goldsmithing techniques combined with modern technology, we maintain complete control over quality from raw material to finished piece.

  • Raw gold sourcing and purchasing
  • 18K and 24K gold work
  • Gold alloying and mixing
  • Intricate filigree work
  • Hand-forged designs

Stone Cutting & Setting

We purchase raw precious stones and gemstones directly from mines and trusted sources. Our master gemologists and setters ensure every stone is cut to perfection and securely set to maximize its brilliance.

  • Raw stone sourcing and purchasing
  • Precision stone cutting
  • Prong, bezel, and pave setting
  • Stone certification and grading

Jewelry Repair & Restoration

Restore your treasured pieces to their original glory. We handle everything from simple repairs to complete restorations.

  • Ring resizing and repairs
  • Chain and clasp repairs
  • Stone replacement
  • Polishing and refinishing

Appraisal Services

Professional jewelry appraisal for insurance, estate planning, or resale purposes by certified gemologists.

  • Certified appraisals
  • Insurance documentation
  • Estate valuations
  • Gemstone authentication

Engraving Services

Add a personal touch with our professional engraving service. Perfect for commemorating special occasions.

  • Hand engraving

  • Custom fonts and designs
  • Message personalization
